Get to know us

At CINÉMATO, we are transforming the movie ticket reservation experience by focusing on enhancing customer satisfaction. We understand that audience satisfaction is crucial for cinema attendance, reputation, and profitability. That’s why we’ve developed an intelligent emotion detection system that leverages the latest advancements in text processing and natural language understanding.

Traditionally, cinemas collect customer feedback through paper surveys, suggestion boxes, and online forms, which are often unreliable and slow. Our innovative technology provides precise and real-time analysis of audience reactions, enabling cinemas to gather authentic and instant feedback. With our solution, cinemas can improve customer satisfaction, strengthen their reputation, and optimize their profitability. Join us for an exceptional cinematic experience, where every viewer feels heard and valued. With CINÉMATO, book your movie tickets with confidence and enjoy superior service.
